Our Story

Bridging the gap between finance and storytelling

I have been creating content since before university, long before it was a career path anyone in finance took seriously. Throughout my time at UCL and into my career in financial services, I kept that creative instinct alive.

Working inside the industry, I saw something no one was addressing: the most interesting people in the world, fund partners, founders, dealmakers, were completely invisible to wider audiences. Meanwhile those audiences were hungry for exactly that kind of authentic, behind the scenes content.

"The finance world is living some of the most compelling stories imaginable. Almost none of it is visible."

So I built The Index Agency. The only personal brand agency in the UK built specifically for financial services professionals.

Built for regulated industries

Compliance is part
of our process

We understand the FCA regulatory environment. Our content protocol is designed from the ground up for financial services so you can build a public profile without ever putting the firm at risk.

Every piece of content goes through a structured review before publishing. Sensitive conversations, deal specifics, and commercially confidential information are never captured. Where needed, content is masked in post production before filming goes live.

Selective filming protocol
Sensitive meetings, deal figures, and confidential screens are never captured. We know what to film and what to leave out.
Post production masking
Any MNPI adjacent content visible incidentally is blurred or muted in editing before the review stage. Standard practice, professionally executed.
48 hour client review
Nothing goes live without your sign off. Your compliance team can be included in every review cycle as standard.
FCA aware content strategy
We never stray into investment advice territory. All content is positioned as personal narrative and thought leadership, not market commentary.
